## Things to remember
- Make sure to remember the different notations for the common number sets
	- Natural Numbers
		- ℕ = {1, 2, 3, 4, 5,...}
	- Integers
		- ℤ = {..., -2, -1, 0, 1, 2, ...}
	- Rational Numbers : $$  ℚ = p/q : p,q \in ℤ , q \neq 0 $$
	- Real Numbers
		- ℝ = {-∞, ∞}
	- Irrational Numbers
		- = ℝ \\ ℚ
